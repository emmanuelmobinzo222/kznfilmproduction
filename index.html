Company background

About KZN Film Production

KZN Film Production was formed in 2010 by Sindisiwe Trustworthy Kumalo after several years in the media industry. The company is 100% black and 100% female owned.

From weddings and traditional celebrations to corporates, conferences, and fast-growing film work, the team invests in market research, fair pricing (daily or hourly), and long-term relationships — including strategic ties with KZN municipalities.

Partner with us

Mission: become the leading location scout and servicing company in the province using the latest technology.

Vision: deliver the highest standard of work with the best KZN location picture — film-friendly and client-focused.

Purpose: renew business ethics, employ from the community, and grow toward a full Media House serving national and international productions.
